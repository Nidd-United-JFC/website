Club Shop

New Club Shop

This year, we have been forced to change our kit supplier. However, we have tried hard to source a new kit that closely matches last season’s design to ensure continuity as much as possible.

You only need to purchase a new Match Kit Set if you are new to the club, your team has a new sponsor, or if last season’s kit no longer fits.


There are two options available in the shop:

  1. Match Kit Sets – These are available to order now and include a Match Shirt, Match Shorts, and Socks.
  2. Individual Items (Top, Shorts, Socks) – These are not yet available but will be available to purchase early next week (29th July).

URGENT: ORDER Timelines for Sponsored Teams

Due to lead times of 5 weeks, we need all sponsored teams to have their kits ordered by 31 July 2025. After you order, the committee will need to collate the orders and submit them for you as a batch order. If you do not get your order in by the 31st, an extra fee of £10 will be charged to cover the cost of an individual order and we can not guarantee your kit will be ready for the start of the season.

Prices

  • Match Kit Set – £42 (includes 1 Top, 1 Shorts, 1 Pair of Socks)

    • If your team is sponsored, you will receive a £20 refund via the club shop after the sponsorship funds have been received.

  • Individual Items:
    • Top – £22
    • Shorts – £17
    • Pair of Socks – £9

Delivery

All items will be ordered in bulk to the club and distributed by the coaches.

Sizing Information

To ensure the best fit, please consult our sizing chart, which you can view here: Sizing Guide.

Before You Order

  • Team Name and Squad Number: You'll need to know your team name and allocated squad number to complete your purchase. If you're unsure of your squad number, please confirm with your coach before ordering.
  • Kit Size: You will also need to know the size of the kits you want to order for each player - see the sizing guide here
  • Sponsored Kits: If your team is Match Kit Sponsored, the sponsor branding will be added to the front of your shirt. Once the sponsorship funds are received, you will also receive a refund for the cost of the shirt.
