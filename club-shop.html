Club Shop - Please Read before Ordering Kit

New Club Shop

This year, we have been forced to change our kit supplier. However, we have tried hard to source a new kit that closely matches last season’s design to ensure continuity.

You only need to purchase a new Match Kit Set if you are new to the club, your team has a new sponsor, or if last season’s kit no longer fits.

As a result, there are two options available in the shop:

  • Match Kit Sets – These are available to order now and include a Match Shirt, Match Shorts, and Socks.
  • Individual Items (Top, Shorts, Socks) – These are not yet available but will be available to purchase early next week (29th July).

URGENT: ORDER Timelines for Sponsored Teams

Due to lead times of 5 weeks, we need all sponsored teams to have their kits ordered by 31 August. After you order, the committee will need to collate the orders and submit them for you as a batch order. If you do not get your order in by the 31st, an extra fee of £10 will be charged to cover the cost of an individual order.

Pricing

  • Match Kit Set (includes 1 Top, 1 Shorts, 1 Pair of Socks) – £42
    If your team is sponsored, you will receive a £20 refund via the club shop after the sponsorship funds have been received.
  • Individual Items:
    • Top – £22
    • Shorts – £17
    • Pair of Socks – £9

Delivery

All items will be ordered in bulk to the club and distributed by the coaches.

Sizing Information

To ensure the best fit, please consult our sizing chart, which you can view here: Sizing Guide.

Before You Order

  • Team Name and Squad Number: You'll need to know your team name and allocated squad number to complete your purchase. If you're unsure of your squad number, please confirm with your coach before ordering.
  • Kit Size: You will also need to know the size of the kits you want to order for each player - see the sizing guide here
  • Sponsored Kits: If your team is Match Kit Sponsored, the sponsor branding will be added to the front of your shirt. Once the sponsorship funds are received, you will also receive a refund for the cost of the shirt.

FAQs

Q: We already have kit from last year, do we have to buy different kit?

A: No, you do not need to buy new kit if your current kit still fits. However, please note that this year’s kit is from a different supplier. While we’ve made every effort to match the new kit closely to the previous one, there will be some slight differences in design.

Q: Our team has a sponsored match kit, how do we order?

A: You can still order and pay through the shop as normal. Once your order is placed, you will receive a refund for the cost of one match kit top once the sponsor funds have been received.

Q: Can I purchase individual items?

A: The Match Kit Set offer includes a full kit with a shirt, shorts, and socks at a discounted price. If you don't need a full new kit, you can buy individual items. Please select the 'Match Kit Individual Items' option from the shop front.

Q: How do I know which size to order?

A: Please refer to our sizing chart here to find the best fit for you.

Q: Do I need to provide a squad number when ordering?

A: Yes, you’ll need to provide your team name and allocated squad number at checkout. If you're unsure of your squad number, please confirm with your coach.

Q: What if my team has a sponsor?

A: If your team’s kit is Match Kit Sponsored, the sponsor’s branding will be added to your shirt. Once the sponsorship funds are received, you will receive a refund for the cost of the shirt.

Q: I'm a coach – how do we get spare goalie tops for our team?

A: For spare goalie tops, please refer to our Sponsor & Match Kit Policy for details on how to order additional items for your team.
