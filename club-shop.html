Club Shop

New Club Shop

Our kit supplier now has their own online shop that you can order direct from.

You only need to purchase a new kit if you are new to the club, your team has a new sponsor, or if last season’s kit no longer fits.

Sizing Information

To ensure the best fit, please consult our sizing chart, which you can view here: Sizing Guide.

Delivery

Orders placed with the supplier will be delivered direct to you. Delivery charges are calculated at checkout.

Before You Order

  • Squad Number: You'll need to know your allocated squad number to complete your purchase. If you're unsure of your squad number, please confirm with your coach before ordering. The Squad number needs to be added to the text box labeled 'INITIALS'. For Match Kit: Do put you initials in the box, just your squad number
  • Kit Size: You will also need to know the size of the kits you want to order for each player - see the sizing guide here
  • Sponsored Kits: If your team is Match Kit Sponsored, you need to know your teams sponsor, and select it when ordering your match kit tops. Branding will be added to the front of your shirt.

FAQs

Q: Why is the refund for a sponsored kit £20, when the full kit costs more?

A:The £20 refund represents roughly a 50/50 split of the sponsor income. We want to make sure that the shirt is effectively "free" for parents, giving them a direct benefit from the sponsorship. However, the remaining amount helps cover costs to ensure the club doesn't run out of funds and doesn't have to raise fees.

Q: We already have kit from last year, do we have to buy different kit?

A: No, you do not need to buy new kit if your current kit still fits. However, please note that this year’s kit is from a different supplier. While we’ve made every effort to match the new kit closely to the previous one, there will be some slight differences in design.

Q: Our team has a sponsored match kit, how do we order?

A: You can still order and pay through the shop as normal just select the correct sponsor when ordering your shirt. Once your order is placed, you will receive a refund from the Club Treasure (NOT THE SUPPLIER) of £20 once the sponsor funds have been received.

Q: Can I purchase individual items?

A: You can buy any individual items from the shop.

Q: How do I know which size to order?

A: Please refer to our sizing chart here to find the best fit for you.

Q: Do I need to provide a squad number when ordering?

A: Yes, you’ll need to provide your allocated squad number and put it in the box labeled 'INITIALS'. If you're unsure of your squad number, please confirm with your coach.
