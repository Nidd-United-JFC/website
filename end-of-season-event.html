End of Season Celebration

We are now able to confirm final details for our end of season celebration.

Venue Details

Venue: Bishop Monkton Playing Fields, HG3 3TN
Date: Sunday 29th June
Time: 1:30pm to 5:30pm
There is parking at the venue as well as on-street parking nearby.

Entertainment

The Nidd Cup – We will be mixing up our 7 teams and holding a friendly mini football tournament to keep the boys entertained.
Please could all players attending wear footy kit, boots, shin pads etc. Nidd kit is preferable but bibs will be provided.

Food & Drink

  • BBQ by the Feasty Boys courtesy of the club.
  • Tuck Shop selling sweets and soft drinks.
  • Bake Sale – HELP PLEASE: We need homemade cake to sell please. If you're a keen baker, anything you can rustle up will be gratefully received. All proceeds go to the club.
  • You are also welcome to bring your own food & drink, including beer & wine. We just respectfully ask that you take your rubbish home with you.

Raffle

HELP PLEASE – If anyone is able to donate a prize to the raffle, this will again be gratefully received. We're thinking bottles of wine, tins of biscuits, that sort of thing. Again, proceeds go to the club.

****Cash is preferable.**** We hope to have a card machine available for payment, but cash always makes things easier and avoids technical hitches.

End of Season Awards

We are here to celebrate the boys' achievements over the season. Before you go home, the Players' Player award will be presented to each team’s chosen player.
Then the boys will split into their teams and their coaches will present the awards and medals for the season.

Please keep your fingers crossed for dry weather and we look forward to seeing you in less than two weeks.

Kind Regards,
Mike Preece
Club Secretary
