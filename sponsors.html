Sponsor the Club

Our Club could not function without the fantastic support and contributions given by our generous sponsors. Private sponsorship is a major source of funding for us and we achieve so much more as a Club because of the generosity of local business and individuals willing to invest in the club.

Make a one-off donation

If you are not able to sponsor the club via one of the packages above, but would still like to financial support what we do, you can give any amount via our secure online giving portal here https://lovegiving.co.uk/nidd-utd/nidd-utd-jfc. Thank you.

All funds donated will be used to help deliver the objectives of Nidd United JFC.
